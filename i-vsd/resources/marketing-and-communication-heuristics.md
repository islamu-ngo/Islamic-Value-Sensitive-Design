# Marketing And Communication Heuristics

## Heuristics

- Make claims truthful, bounded, and evidence-backed.
- Keep a claim-evidence register.
- Respect user attention and consent.
- Use opt-in for nonessential marketing.
- Make unsubscribe easy.
- Avoid guilt, fear, scarcity, or spiritual manipulation.
- Communicate material changes before they harm trust.
- Compare honestly.

## Comparison Requirements

- Date comparisons and state assumptions.
- Acknowledge competitor strengths and limits of your own product.
- Avoid checkmark flattening and misleading category framing.
- Explain who should not choose the product.
- Avoid negative attacks, fake reviews, fake users, fake authority, and manufactured community proof.

## Evidence Expectations

Marketing copy, screenshots, emails, claims register, endorsements provenance, comparison methodology, pricing terms, changelog, and support complaints.

## Anti-Patterns

AI washing, exaggerated privacy/security, false open-source association, roadmap-as-current claims, guilt prompts, spiritual fear, hidden fees, and unclear material changes.
