# Legal And Compliance Framework

## Complementary Standards

Use GDPR or equivalent privacy principles, accessibility standards, consumer protection rules, security obligations, and platform policies when aligned with I-VSD. They can operationalize duties around access, deletion, transparency, reasonable terms, and data-use disclosure.

## Review Areas

- Access, correction, deletion, export, and portability rights.
- Plain-language, reasonable, non-hidden terms.
- Clear communication of material changes before harm.
- Transparent data use, sharing, retention, and processing location.
- No hidden fees, hidden limits, or hidden provider powers.
- Records showing legal compliance and ethical review are both considered.

## Boundary

Legal compliance is not moral sufficiency. A practice may be legal but still deceptive, exploitative, harmful, immodest, surveillance-oriented, or uncertain under Islamic principles.

## Escalation

Use legal counsel for statutory, contractual, liability, employment, consumer-protection, privacy, IP, or jurisdictional questions. Use qualified Islamic scholarly review for fatwa, halal/haram, Sharia, riba, religious-content, or jurisprudential questions. Some cases require both.
