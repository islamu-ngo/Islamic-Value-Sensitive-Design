# Evaluation Metrics

Use metrics to detect whether responsibilities are being honored. Metrics support operational validation only when collected, reviewed, and acted on.

## Metrics Beyond Revenue

- Principle-review completion and unresolved ethical risks.
- User complaints, harm reports, and trust indicators.
- Appeal volume, appeal outcomes, and correction time.
- Support burden, response times, and respectful communication quality.
- Privacy/security incidents, audit results, and vulnerability response.
- Accessibility findings and remediation time.
- Portability/export success, deletion completion, and migration outcomes.
- Moderation consistency, reversal rates, and abuse handling.
- Backup restore tests and incident response times.
- Sustainability, maintenance signals, dependency risk, and EOL readiness.

## Warnings

Barakah is theological and not a measurable KPI. Engagement, conversion, retention, and revenue are not moral success by themselves and may signal harm when driven by addiction, confusion, surveillance, or lock-in.

## Output Rule

For each metric, state owner, source, review cadence, action threshold, affected stakeholders, and limitations.
